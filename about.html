About Us

French Luxury, Made Affordable

You no longer need to worry about the increasing costs of travel acommodation.

20+ Years of Class and Luxury

Founded in 2000 and renovated in its entirety in 2015, the main goal of the Grand Hotel Aeonia is to offer its guests a luxurious and classy accommodation experience, without the hefty price tag it usually comes attached with.

Our guests can look forward to everything that one would expect from a hotel such as ours, from a variety of fully-equipped rooms and our own in-house restaurant, to free Wi-Fi and a great location here in Paris.
